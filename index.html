Background

I'm a big nerd at heart and entirely fervent about what I do, I love the laws of old-school branding (think 1950s Coca-Cola, or Adidas or Kool-Aid), where there was no denying the connection and steadfast loyalty people felt toward their brands of choice.

Keen to bring back traditional techniques, I began Chirp Media, Inc., 16 years ago, to embody mediums that would evoke emotional brand stories while also embracing the digital age.
